# RMD-Snippets
RMD snipets for Prinses Maxima Centrum

# What is it?

These RDM snippets provide the content you need to stay in touch with your research community. 

RDM snippets are practical tips on research data management that can be read in under 20 sec. Highly accessible bits of information that researchers can coincidentally bump into during the workday.

# Why is it?

Between trainings and requests for help, we often lose track of researchers. To keep awareness of good RDM practices high, we need more frequent proactive communication. 

# In what context to use it?

Mailing, intranet item, social post, mail signature, …

# How to use it?

Are you looking for tips to share with your research community? Do you want to help them to keep good data management in mind? If so, take a look at the info snippets and CTRL+C, CTRL+V whatever matches the interest of your research community.

Balance the content of the info snippets you’re sharing using the columns on FAIR principles and Data Life Cycle phases. 

The columns Research_domain and Organisation_specific  indicate that there is a primary audience for the info snippet. With a bit of editing, you might find that the RDM snippet can be used for other or more general audiences.

# How to contribute to the collection?

Do you have a link to a great resource that you’re always sharing with researchers? Or a nice article that convinces people to think about RDM?

Your input is valued! Share your RDM snippet with the data management support community. 

To keep the info snippets snappy, the character limit has been set to 250. Adding a URL for further reading is encouraged.
